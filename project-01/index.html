ABOUT ME

I am an Access Services representative in the Baylor Scott and White Hospital System. I am passionate about people and taking care of them in the most efficient and informed way possible. In my free time I love too read, study entomology, junk journal. I also enjoy playing board games, video games, and roleplaying games. My previous positions include working in manufacturing at Kreate, food safety sanitation at HEB, as well as veterinary medicine at Parmer Lane Pet Hospital.
